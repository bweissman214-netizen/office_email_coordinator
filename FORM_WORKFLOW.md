# Form-Based Qualification Workflow

## Overview

Simplified workflow: When a speaking inquiry arrives, send a single Google Form that captures all qualification data in one step. Eliminates back-and-forth emails.

## The Form

**URL:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfE1atyYdLtQIaiUGFhT2z9Mt-61FQN5OYvuug3KpxOrXD6IA/viewform

**Fields:**
1. Date/Timing (date input)
2. Location/City (text input)
3. Budget (text input)
4. Event Type (multiple choice: Corporate/Nonprofit)
5. Audience Size (multiple choice: <20 / 20-100 / >100)
6. Special Requirements (text input)

## Workflow

**Step 1: Detect Speaking Inquiry**
- Monitor blake@backpackvc.com for unread emails
- Look for speaking-related keywords (speak, speaking, event, etc.)

**Step 2: Send Form Link**
- Create draft from bweissman214@gmail.com
- Subject: "Re: [original subject]"
- Body: 
  ```
  Thanks for reaching out about speaking! 
  
  To help us qualify your request, please fill out this brief form:
  [FORM LINK]
  
  Once we receive your response, we'll follow up with next steps.
  
  Best regards,
  Charlie Harary's Office
  ```
- User reviews and sends

**Step 3: Form Submission**
- Organizer fills out form
- All data captured in one submission

**Step 4: Process Form Data**
- Monitor Google Form responses
- Extract: date, location, budget, event type, audience, requirements
- Proceed with next workflow steps (accommodations, flights, etc.)
